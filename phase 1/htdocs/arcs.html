Gintama has a wide variety of genres. At one moment, your laughing non-stop; the next thing you know, you are already crying. Here are some of the remarkable arcs that make up the series.




Benizakura

A mysterious man is going around and cutting down innocent people. The Yorozuya receives two requests, one from Elizabeth, another from Tetsuya. This arc escalates into introducing the Kiheitai and gives us a quick glimpse of Gintoki, Takasugi, and Katsura's history with a man named Shoyo.



Shinsengumi Crisis

Hijikata Toshiro, the Demon Vice-Captain of the Shinsengumi, starts acting a bit off. Itou Kamotarou, the Shinsengumi adviser and a new character, enters the scene with a suspicious intent.



Yoshiwara in Flames

Gintoki bumps into a troublesome, pickpocketing kid. They then find out the real intentions of the young boy, heading to the underground red-light district called the Yoshiwara.



Kabukicho Four Devas

A girl named Chin Pirako introduces herself to the Yorozuya. She then tells them that she's looking for the strongest man in the Kabuki District. Internal conflict broke out in the four devas and, things didn't turn out smoothly for the Yorozuya and the staff of Snack Otose.



Courtesan of the Nation

Suzuran, Yoshiwara's most beautiful courtesan, requested to see Gintoki to thank him for saving Yoshiwara. It turns out that Suzuran was already an old lady and was waiting for a person she loved who made a promise to see her on the next full moon. Gintoki and Tsukuyo decided to look for this man to help fulfill that promise.



Shogun Assassination

Nobu Nobu, the Kiheitai, and the Harusame are planning for the Shogun's Assassination. To protect Shige Shige, Matsudaira generates a plan involving the help of the Yorozuya, Oniwabanshuu, and Shinsengumi. The Shogun Assassination Arc is one of Gintama's most, if not the best, well-done arcs in the series.



Farewell Shinsengumi

Due to an unfortunate event, both Matsudaira and Kondo Isao were arrested and sentenced to death, leaving the Shinsengumi to disband. Two rivaling organizations, the Jouishishi and the disbanded Shinsengumi plus the Yorozuya, teamed up to save Matsudaira, Kondo, and the captured leader of the Jouishishi, Katsura.



Silver Soul

The main antagonist of the series, Utsuro, finally displays his true intentions. This arc sends out nostalgic feels as Gintoki, his other allies, and some former enemies from all around the series team up to stop Utsuro's plans.
