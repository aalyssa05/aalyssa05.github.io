For those that don't know Gintama:

Gintama is a Japanese manga that is written and illustrated by Hideaki Sorachi. Its storyline begins with the Amantos descending from space 20 years ago. The nation that used to be called "The Country of the Samurai," where brave men that were the samurai once freely strolled around, was now filled with swaggering aliens. Due to this Amanto invasion and the sword ban, the numbers of the samurai declined.

Despite this, one man remained with the samurai spirit. This man's name is Sakata Gintoki, a sweet-tooth and reckless man. Together with Shimura Shinpachi, a boy with glasses and a samurai in training, Kagura, a member of the Yato tribe, and also Sadaharu, their overside pet dog and mascot, all four of them make up the Yorozuya.

The anime includes the genres of action, comedy, historical, parody, samurai, sci-fi, and shounen. Gintama's anime series first aired on April 4, 2006, and the latest episode aired on October 8, 2018. Sunrise handled the anime series from the very first episode until episode 265. The remaining episodes, 266 to 367, were handled by Bandai Namco Studios. Sadly, the manga series ended last year in July, but a final movie was announced and is to be released next year.





Author's Note:




A rollercoaster ride, this project took me on a rollercoaster ride. I was either really frustrated or in glee while coding the pages. The moments of going back and forth from Sublime to Chrome will be the highlight of my experience. This type of work may not be my forte, but I have learned a lot throughout the making of the project.

I am grateful to be able to make a website based on one of my favorite series. It might not be the most creative or the prettiest, but it was fun making. I hope you weren't bored skimming through the pages. 'Til next time!
